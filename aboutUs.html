Situated in the bustling city of Jakarta, Indonesia, JCC (Jockey's Conference Center) has been a center of excellence and a premier event destination since its inception in 1974.

Our History:

With a history spanning nearly half a century, JCC has been a landmark venue for many significant national and international events. From the time we opened our doors in 1974, we have set a high standard for hosting a variety of events that resonate far beyond our locale. A significant milestone in our journey was hosting the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference. This notable event placed JCC on the global stage, highlighting our capability to facilitate dialogues of immense geopolitical significance. Moreover, our role has extended beyond politics and diplomacy. We have been a driving force in Jakarta's economic landscape, providing a platform for industry exhibitions, trade fairs, and other business events. These events have helped promote local businesses, foster international collaborations, and stimulate economic growth. JCC's scope is not limited to business and politics. We've also been a vibrant hub for cultural events, including indoor sports competitions and musical concerts, playing a key role in enriching the cultural fabric of Jakarta.

Our Certifications:

Our commitment to quality is showcased by our ISO 9001 certification, a recognition by the International Organization for Standardization. This certification affirms our dedication to maintaining a robust quality management system that consistently meets our customers' needs and is focused on continual service improvement. Our sustainability efforts are recognized through the Green Building Certification. This credential acknowledges our dedication to reducing our environmental footprint, enhancing energy efficiency, and adopting waste management practices that prioritize environmental conservation. Safety is of paramount importance to us at JCC, and our Venue Safety and Security Certification is a testament to this commitment. We adhere to rigorous safety standards, providing a secure and welcoming environment for all attendees and staff.

Our Mission:

At JCC, our mission is to create unforgettable experiences by offering unrivaled service quality. Our dedicated team of professionals works diligently to ensure every event we host is a resounding success. As we look towards the future, we continue to uphold our commitment to customer satisfaction, sustainability, and continuous improvement. We are excited to partner with you to create more memorable experiences. Thank you for considering JCC as your event partner. Your trust in us fuels our drive for excellence.
