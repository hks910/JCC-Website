Who Are We?
JCC is a famous conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. JCC is one of the earliest as well as popular convention centers in Jakarta. First opened in 1974, JCC many important national and international conference, exhibition, fair, indoor sports, and musical concerts were held at JCC including 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and Asian-African Conference. It is a very venue place throughout the year. JCC stands as a testament to Jakarta's vibrant spirit and its dedication to fostering intellectual exchange, cultural appreciation, and artistic exploration. With its timeless charm, unparalleled services, and an unwavering dedication to hosting remarkable events, JCC continues to be a beacon of inspiration and a hallmark of excellence.
